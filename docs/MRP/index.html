Executive Summary

Meta Rational Pragmatics is not organized here as a loose philosophical appendix. The central research focus of this track is the construction and refinement of MRP-VM: a runtime for governed interpretation, explicit routes between execution regimes, auditable frames, and the gradual conversion of broad neural competence into more structured and reusable forms.

This matters because we want a path toward AI that is more reliable, easier for firms to use, and less dependent on permanently expensive inference. The scientific ambition is clear: bring advanced AI closer to ordinary computers, make it more trustworthy in enterprise settings, and extend it toward serious research automation rather than treating current LLM behavior as the final architecture.

Primary Object

MRP-VM as a runtime for governed interpretation, active frames, explicit routes, regime selection, and auditable execution.

Deployment Goal

Trustworthy local AI that becomes easier to operate in companies, more legible under audit, and more compatible with ordinary-computer deployment profiles.

Translation Logic

A fundable research agenda with visible milestones, direct enterprise relevance, and a credible path toward research automation as the next logical step after the LLM phase.

Research Focus

The majority of the research energy in this series is intended to flow into MRP-VM itself. We want to open new research projects, funding proposals, and investor conversations around it because it describes a concrete technical solution rather than a vague AI ambition. It gives a coherent object of work around which theory, engineering, benchmarking, and translation can all accumulate.

In practical terms, MRP-VM is where we can unify agent skills, explicit decomposition, regime selection, typed intermediates, validators, bounded workflows, smaller local execution units, and controlled use of language models inside one architecture. That makes it the clearest bridge between rigorous research and systems that firms can actually trust and use.

Why This Is Researchable

MRP-VM breaks down into explicit questions about runtime design, routing, symbolic and local execution, compiled skills, validation, provenance, and tractable interpreters.

Why This Is Fundable

The program is technically legible, stageable, and measurable. It can support grants, partnerships, and investment because it offers clear milestones rather than generic AGI rhetoric.

Strategic thesis: MRP-VM is the place where this series stops being only interpretive and becomes a build agenda for trustworthy, local, enterprise-ready AI.

Why MRP-VM Matters

We are interested in MRP-VM because it offers a path toward AI on ordinary computers in a technically serious sense: not by shrinking intelligence into a slogan, but by moving more of the burden into explicit skills, validators, symbolic fragments, compact learned modules, and regime-aware routing.

If that transition works, advanced AI becomes easier to trust, easier to operate, and easier for companies to adopt. It becomes more compatible with CPU-friendly deployment profiles, with auditable enterprise automation, and with research workflows where correctness, provenance, and controlled execution matter as much as fluency.

That is also why this looks like the next logical step. The LLM era showed the power of language-mediated intelligence. The next phase is to reorganize that power into systems that are more modular, more stable, more usable, and more computationally real.

Program Table

The research program can be read as a set of concrete work packages rather than as a single abstract vision.

Track What Gets Built Why It Matters
MRP-VM Kernel Goals, frames, routes, plugins, epochs, provenance, and auditable runtime control. Turns MRP into a concrete scientific and engineering object rather than a label.
Skills and Typed Intermediates Explicit skill contracts, validators, reusable intermediate artefacts, and bounded workflows. Improves legibility, controllability, and systematic reuse across domains.
Regime Selection Mechanisms for choosing between symbolic, retrieval, graph, learned, and hybrid execution. Moves intelligence from one opaque regime toward governed plurality.
Local and Trustworthy Execution Smaller modules, symbolic fragments, caches, compact routers, and CPU-friendly execution paths. Supports reliable industrial deployment and reduces dependence on permanently expensive inference.
Research Automation Systems that can assist literature work, analysis, synthesis, protocol handling, and structured inquiry. Extends the program from enterprise automation toward serious augmentation of research itself.
