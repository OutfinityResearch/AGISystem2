Trustworthy AI

Back to index | Back to concepts

Trustworthy AI is an umbrella term for systems that are reliable, transparent and aligned with human values; this project contributes by making reasoning deterministic, geometrically interpretable and auditable through explicit bias and validation mechanisms.

In AGISystem2

The system is designed from the ground up to be trustworthy, with built-in mechanisms for transparency, accountability, and value alignment.

Key trustworthy AI features:

  • Determinism - All reasoning processes are reproducible and predictable
  • Transparency - Every decision can be explained through geometric operations
  • Auditability - Complete provenance tracking for all reasoning steps
  • Value alignment - Explicit representation and management of values

Technical Implementation

Trustworthiness is implemented through multiple system components: the ValidationEngine ensures correctness, the BiasController manages value alignment, the AuditLog provides complete provenance tracking, and the geometric nature of all reasoning ensures transparency and explainability.
