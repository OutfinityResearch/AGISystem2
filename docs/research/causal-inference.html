Causal Inference

Enabling AI to ask "Why?" and "What if?" through causal reasoning.

The Ladder of Causation

Championed by Judea Pearl, Causal Inference moves AI from curve-fitting (correlations) to understanding mechanisms (causality). This is essential for true System 2 Reasoning.

The Three Levels

  1. Association (System 1): What does one thing tell me about another? (e.g., correlations in LLMs).
  2. Intervention: What happens if I change something? (e.g., the do-calculus).
  3. Counterfactuals: Why did it happen? What if I had acted differently?

Strategic Goal

Standard LLMs are stuck at Level 1. In AGISystem2, we integrate Structural Causal Models (SCMs) to allow agents to reason about the consequences of their actions and explain their decisions in terms of cause and effect.
