Neuro-Symbolic AI

Combining the pattern recognition of neural networks with the logic of symbolic AI.

The Third Wave of AI

Neuro-symbolic AI seeks to combine the strengths of Connectionism (Neural Networks) and Symbolism (Logic and Rules). This is often described as the merger of System 1 (fast, intuitive) and System 2 (slow, logical).

Key Hybrid Approaches

  • Symbolic Front-ends: Using CNLs to translate human intent into logic, which is then parsed by neural networks.
  • Differentiable Logic: Frameworks that allow backpropagation through logical operations (e.g., Logical Neural Networks).
  • Geometric Reasoning: Using VSA/HDC to perform symbolic manipulations in a vector space.

Why it Matters for AGISystem2

The core bottleneck of modern LLMs is the lack of a "grounded" logical world model. By integrating symbolic constraints, we can build agents that don't just "guess" the next word, but reason about Consistency, Causality, and Proof.
