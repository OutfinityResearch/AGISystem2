High-Fidelity CPU Runtimes for Emerging Markets.

Ziroh Labs and Kompact AI

Based in India, Ziroh Labs developed Kompact AI to break the GPU barrier for emerging markets. Unlike quantization-focused efforts, they prioritize high fidelity.

Full-Precision (BF16) Performance

While many local inference techniques degrade precision to 4-bit, Kompact AI runs models at Full Precision (BF16 - Brain Floating Point 16) through a runtime that manages memory and compute cycles with extreme efficiency. This is critical for legal or medical fields where quantization errors are unacceptable.

"Elephant" Semantic Cache

A key innovation is the Elephant semantic caching layer, which detects semantic similarities between queries to skip redundant inference cycles.

Result: Processing speeds of 164 tokens/sec (comparable to an NVIDIA A100) and peaks of 10,000 tokens/sec for repetitive tasks.

Geopolitics and AI Sovereignty

As export controls on advanced accelerators tighten, Kompact AI provides a path to build nation-grade AI infrastructure using commodity CPU servers (x86 or ARM) that are commercially available and not subject to sanctions.
